Chicken Curry

A Delicious Chicken Curry Recipe

You don't need to leave the house to enjoy satisfying Indian chicken curry. With this recipe, you can have a

restaurant-worthy meal on the table in under an hour.

Ingredients
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until lightly browned. Stir

    garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt. Continue

    stirring for 2 minutes. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk.

    Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.

  2. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more

    minutes.
